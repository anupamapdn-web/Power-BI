The goal of the dashboard is to uncover patterns and factors contributing to employee turnover, and to provide actionable insights.
The analysis of employee attrition reveals that department, job level, age, overtime, and marital status are key influencing factors. By focusing on these areas, IBM can implement targeted strategies to reduce turnover and enhance employee satisfaction
